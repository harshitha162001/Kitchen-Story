Kitchen Story is an Indian online food ordering and delivery platform.... Founded in 2014, website is based in Bangalore and operates in 500 Indian cities as of September 2021 Besides food delivery, Kitchenstory also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart, and a same-day package delivery service called Kitchenstory Genie.

Kitchen story has partnered with Burger King to provide delivery services.
